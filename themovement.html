THE MOVEMENT

COUP is more than a framework, course or workshop. COUP is a movement. Everyone who completes the COUP course and masters the COUP framework becomes part of a global movement.

OUR MISSION

Making presentations greatagain for the first time — for presenters and audiences.

We can’t stand to see any more people and products wasting their potential due to bad presentation skills. We can’t stand to see any more people bored to death during presentations.

We want will change presentation culturein corporations, agencies, startups, universities, institutions ... everywhere.

Members of the COUP Movement help each other. We’re organized into a global community that gives feedback to presentations of other members and supports each other to become better presenters. Besides our digital community, we also plan to establish physical events.

Join COUP now and become a part of the biggest presentation movement in the world.
