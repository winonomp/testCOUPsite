ONLINE COURSE

overview

The COUP Movement is based on a power course:

TARGET GROUP

EVERY single human that gives internal or external presentations and speeches.

  • Executves
  • Employees (Startup or Corporate
  • Salespeople
  • Founders/Entrepreneurs
  • Developers (giving Tech Talks)
  • Students
  • Scientists
  • Educators
  • ...EVERYONE!

TARGETS

  • Enable every individual and team to be more successful through impactful, effective and outstanding (verbal) presentations.
  • Increase sales, pitching and marketing success, improve productivity, enhance internal and external communication effectiveness and shareholder value.
  • Have fun while presenting!

The COUP Framework not only helps you deliver outstanding public presentations and speeches, but it will also help you in every situation where you need to convey messages and ideas effectively: Customer pitches, project meetings, internal meetings, lectures, and one-on-one communication.

The COUP Framework will provide you with a universal toolset to establish your ideas and thoughts in the minds of your audience.
